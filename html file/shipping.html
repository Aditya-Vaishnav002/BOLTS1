Shipping & Returns Policy

Everything you need to know about getting your order fast and our easy return process.

Shipping Information

Standard Delivery

Delivery time: **3-7 business days** (depending on location). Cost: **$5.00** or **FREE** on orders over $50.

Express Delivery

Delivery time: **1-2 business days**. Cost: **$15.00**.

Our Returns Policy

We accept returns for a full refund within **30 days** of delivery. Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and include all tags. Sale items are eligible for store credit only.

To initiate a return, please visit our Online Returns Portal.
